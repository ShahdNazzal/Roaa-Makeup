Meet the Artist

Biography

Roaa is a visionary makeup artist dedicated to the art of luxury beauty and sophisticated transformations. With an unwavering eye for detail and a passion for enhancing natural features, she has established herself as a premiere choice for women seeking an elevated aesthetic.

Her philosophy centers on the belief that makeup should not only look flawless in person and on camera but should also empower the individual with a sense of timeless elegance. Roaa combines modern techniques with the highest quality products to create looks that are as enduring as they are exquisite, ensuring every client walks away feeling like the most radiant version of themselves.
